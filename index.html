Receipt Printing

When on, the checkout will print a receipt before recording the sale. Turn this off to push transactions through without printing.

Confirm before finalizing

After printing, ask "Did the receipt print?" before recording the sale. Turn this on if your printer is unreliable — it gives you a chance to reprint before the transaction is saved.

Print 2 Copies

Print a customer copy and a business copy for every sale.

Dine In / Takeout

When on, staff can choose the order type on each transaction. Turn this off for service businesses where the distinction doesn't apply.

Show removed adjustments on receipt

When on, removed line items appear crossed out on the receipt.
